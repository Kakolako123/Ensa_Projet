About ENSA Marrakech

More than 20 years of excellence

The ENSA of Marrakech is a public institution of higher education belonging to Cadi Ayyad University. It is a public actor that plays a leadership role in high-value-added engineering education programs, ensuring quality training and skills development, built on shared commitment between engineering students, professors, and administrative and technical staff. Since its establishment in 2000, ENSA Marrakech has trained high-quality state engineers Today, they hold key positions in companies in Morocco and abroad, particularly in the service and industry sectors. Our mission is to train the engineers of tomorrow, through active pedagogy based on professional, international, and cultural openness. Our goal is to develop an entrepreneurial mindset, transforming students into key actors in their professional and cultural development. Our motto is to innovate to succeed.
